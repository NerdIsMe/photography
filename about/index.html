嗨，我是許哲！

我即將畢業於台大資管系，在大四上就修完學分，但拖到大五上才要畢業。
我喜歡用鏡頭捕捉旅遊的精彩，也喜歡分享旅途的美景。

Hello, this is Jerome!

I will soon graduate from the Department of Information Management at National Taiwan University. I enjoy capturing the beauty of travel with my camera and sharing the fabulous scenery of my journeys.

如何踏入攝影的？

大三下一開學，我聽到系上的學長姊因為大四威能，初選選到森多概，非常羨慕。然後我就碰碰運氣關注森多概的加簽方式，就順利加簽到啦！為了捕捉五天四夜在台大實驗林的畫面，我和學長借了 Canon 70D，就這樣開啟了攝影之旅，掉入攝影這的大錢坑 XD

  • P.S. 森多概全名「森林生物多樣性概論」，是一門台大的通識兼選修課程，在台大實驗林進行五天四夜的生態校外教學。

How did I get started in Photography?

During my junior year, my friends were able to successfully sign up for the “Introduction to Forest Biodiversity” course due to their higher grades, which made me very envious. I decided to take a chance and follow the sign-up process for it, and I was accepted luckily! To document the five-day field trip to the National Taiwan University Experimental Forest, I borrowed a Canon 70D camera and embarked on my journey as an amateur photographer while traveling.
